---

## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. Syllabus Micro-Tasking & Tree View

* **Granular Tracking:** Breakdown of GS (I-IV) and Prelims into tiny sub-topics.
* **Progress States:** `Not Started`, `In Progress`, `Completed`, `Revised`.
* **Resource Mapping:** Direct links to Unacademy chapters, NCERT PDFs, and YouTube lectures for every topic.

### 2. The "Spaced Repetition" Engine

* **Auto-Scheduler:** Automatically sets revision dates (1-7-30 days) upon topic completion.
* **Confidence Scoring:** 1-5 star rating system to prioritize weak areas for AI-suggested deep dives.

### 3. Current Affairs & Static Linker

* **Daily Log:** Tag news headlines directly to GS syllabus sections (e.g., GS-II Polity).
* **Monthly Milestone Tracker:** Monitor progress on monthly compilations (Vision/Unacademy).

### 4. Answer Writing & PYQ Vault

* **Writing Streaks:** Daily counters for Mains answer practice.
* **Image Uploads:** Upload photos of handwritten answers to Firebase Storage for digital archiving.

### 5. AI Assistant (Gemini-Powered)

* **Syllabus Breakdown:** Use AI to split broad topics into micro-tasks.
* **Concept Simplification:** Ask the built-in AI to explain complex topics like "Fiscal Drag" or "Joint Sitting" instantly.
